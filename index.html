Who We Are

Sharon Fellowship Church in Thykoodam is a warm, Bible-believing fellowship dedicated to leading people into a growing relationship with Jesus Christ.

Whether you are seeking answers, looking for a spiritual home, or wanting to anchor your family in truth, there is a place reserved here for you.
